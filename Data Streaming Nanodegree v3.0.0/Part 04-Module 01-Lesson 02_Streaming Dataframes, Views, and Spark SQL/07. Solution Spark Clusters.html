Spark Cluster Review

To take advantage of the resources in the Spark cluster, we submitted (or deployed) the Spark code:

  1. Started the Spark master
  2. Started the Spark workers
  3. Submitted your application

$ /home/workspace/spark/sbin/start-slave.sh spark://719f72471d5b:7077
