04. Meet Your Instructors

Ben Goldberg is our Instructor for the Data Ingestion with Kafka and Kafka Streaming course. In his career as an engineer, Ben has worked in fields ranging from Computer Vision to Natural Language Processing. At SpotHero, he founded and built out their Data Engineering team, using Airflow as one of the key technologies. Ben has also taught a course in our Data Engineering Nanodegree program as well!

Jillian Kim teaches our Spark Streaming course for this program. Jillian is a Senior Data Engineer at Change Healthcare where she has worked in roles from building data analytics platforms to machine learning pipelines. Previously, she was a research engineer at Samsung focused on data analytics and ML, and now leads building pipelines at scale as a Senior Data Engineer at Change Healthcare.

David Drummond is the VP of Engineering at Insight where he enjoys breaking down difficult concepts and helping others learn data engineering. David has a PhD in Physics from UC Riverside. David and Judit (bio below) have also helped build our Data Scientist Nanodegree program!

Judit Lantos is a Senior Data Engineer at Netflix. Formerly she was a Data Engineer at Split, where she worked on the statistical engine of their full-stake experimentation platform, she has also been an instructor at Insight Data Science, helping software engineers and academic coders transition to DE roles. Judit has helped build content for our Data Scientist Nanodegree program!
